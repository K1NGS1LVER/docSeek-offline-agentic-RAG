How does hybrid search combine dense and keyword results?
planconceptual query → k=6 · rerank on
retrieve18 candidates · dense + bm25 · rrf-fused
rerankcross-encoder · 18 → 6
gradeevidence sufficient · confidence 0.82

Hybrid search runs two retrievals in parallel: dense vector search over FAISS embeddings catches meaning, while BM25 keyword search over the FTS5 index catches exact terms1. The two ranked lists are then merged with reciprocal rank fusion, so a chunk that scores well in either list rises to the top2.

Weak dense-only hits below the similarity threshold are dropped — unless they also matched keywords, in which case they are kept regardless of score1.
